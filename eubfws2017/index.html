Choosing vocabs

  1. DC Terms
  2. Bibframe 2.0
  3. Bibliograpic Ontology (Bibo)
  4. Resource Description and Access (RDA) Unconstrained Properties
  5. schema.org
  6. Other vocabularies (MADS, Music Ontology, DC Elements,…) for individual elements
  7. Our own vocabulary
